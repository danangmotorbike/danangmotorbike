Rental Process

  1. Pre-booking Contact: Via phone or website, specify the motorbike type and pick-up/return dates.
  2. Confirmation and Deposit: Typically requires a deposit of 500,000-1,000,000 VND or National ID card/Citizen card as collateral.
  3. Motorbike Inspection: Customer and staff jointly check the appearance, brakes, lights, tires, fuel, and helmets.
  4. Contract Signing: Clear agreement on price, duration, and repair responsibilities (in case of minor incidents).
  5. Return: Return on time to receive back your deposit and identification documents.
